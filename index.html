Introduction

Welcome to the Nintendo Switch homebrew guide! This guide will walk you through the process of setting up homebrew on your Nintendo Switch. Please follow the instructions carefully.

Requirements

Before getting started, make sure you have the following:

  • Nintendo Switch (must be hackable)
  • SD card (at least 32GB recommended)
  • Internet connection
  • Homebrew software (such as Atmosphère)

Steps

Here are the steps to set up homebrew on your Switch:

  1. Backup your Switch's NAND.
  2. Download and install the Atmosphère custom firmware.
  3. Place the required files on your SD card.
  4. Boot into RCM and run the payload.
  5. Launch the homebrew menu from the Switch.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about Switch homebrewing:

Is homebrewing safe?

Homebrewing has risks, such as voiding your warranty or bricking your device. Be cautious and follow guides carefully.

Can I go online with homebrew?

Going online with homebrew can result in a ban from Nintendo. It's recommended to use a second Switch for homebrew purposes.
