### Mariko [ and patched Erista ]

---

:::note[Mariko users]
Please ensure that your desired payload supports your device.
:::

1. Make sure you have Hekate installed on your device.
    - If you have SX Gear, first follow [SXOS Migration](/miscellaneous/sxos).
2. Rename ```hekate_ctcaer_x.x.x.bin``` to ```payload.bin```.
3. Place your desired payload in ```/bootloader/payloads/```.
4. Press the power button and launch into Hekate.
5. Once inside Hekate, press the ```payloads``` option and pick your desired payload.
6. You should now launch into your desired payload.
