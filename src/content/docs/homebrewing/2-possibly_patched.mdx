### How to check
:::note[Possible outcomes]
There are **2 outcomes** from this. You will find out whether your V1 Switch is **patched** or **unpatched**
:::

First, follow the guide for [Unpatched V1](/homebrewing/unpatched_v1/) like normal:

- If the injection is successful, and you boot into Hekate, your V1 Switch is **unpatched**
- If the injection is successful, but the Switch stay in RCM mode, see the references below to confirm if your V1 Switch is patched or not
    - If it's **patched**, please follow the guide for [Patched](/homebrewing/patched) Switch
